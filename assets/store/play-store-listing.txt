FULL DESCRIPTION  [784 chars]
══════════════════
Plenty turns hydration into a rewarding daily habit.

Track every sip with one tap. A beautiful animated water container fills as you progress toward your personalized daily goal, calculated from your weight and activity level.

Build streaks with consecutive-day logs. A 7-day heatmap shows your consistency at a glance. Use streak freezes to keep your streak alive on off days.

Unlock rewards at milestones: Bronze (3 days), Silver (7), Gold (14), and Diamond (30). Earn achievement badges for streaks, milestones, and habit patterns.

Weather-aware reminders adapt to your local conditions via Open-Meteo — hotter days mean more frequent nudges. Set quiet hours to sleep undisturbed.

All data stays on your device. No accounts, no sign-ups, no cloud sync. Your habits are yours alone.
