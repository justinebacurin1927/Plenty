# Plenty Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-07-19

## Your Data Stays on Your Device

Plenty is built with your privacy as a core principle. The app operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## What Plenty Stores

All data created while using Plenty is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage:

- Your drink logs (timestamps and amounts)
- Your daily goal and settings (reminder interval, quiet hours, theme preference)
- Your achievements and streak data
- Your weight-based goal calculation (optional weight input)

None of this data leaves your device.

## Location Data

If you grant location permission, Plenty uses your approximate location solely to retrieve local weather data from the Open-Meteo API. This allows the app to provide weather-aware hydration reminders — for example, suggesting more frequent drinks on hot days.

- **Location data is used ephemerally.** Your coordinates are sent to Open-Meteo to fetch the local forecast. They are not stored on your device, not saved to AsyncStorage, and not transmitted anywhere else.
- **Location data is never shared** with any third party beyond the Open-Meteo API call, which is a standard weather query with no user identification.
- You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings. The app will fall back to manual city name input for weather data.

## No Analytics

Plenty contains no analytics SDKs, no crash reporting tools, no telemetry, and no tracking code. There is no way for Plenty to know how you use the app, how often you open it, or whether you encounter errors.

## No Accounts

Plenty does not require or support user accounts. There is no sign-up, no login, and no cloud sync. Your data belongs to you and stays on your device.

## No Third-Party Sharing

Plenty does not share any data with third parties. The app has no advertising SDKs, no social media integrations, and no data brokers.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all data at any time by going to **Settings → Data Export & Backup → Reset All Data** within the app. This permanently removes all drink logs, settings, and achievements from your device.

## Children's Privacy

Plenty does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for all ages to use.

## Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored on external servers, there is no risk of server-side data breaches. Your drinking data is as secure as your device itself.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top of this document will be revised. Changes will be minor and will never involve new data collection.
